About Our Initiative

Connecting our community, technology, and city services to build a cleaner, smarter future for everyone.

WHAT WE DO

A New Approach to Urban Cleanliness

The Clean City platform is a software-driven solution designed to revolutionize how our municipality handles waste management. We bridge the gap between citizens, collection crews, and city administration using smart technology.

Instead of relying on expensive hardware or sensors, we empower citizens to be the "eyes" of the city. Through our easy-to-use portal, anyone can report an overflowing bin or a civic issue. Our AI backend then verifies the report, notifies the nearest driver, and optimizes their route, ensuring a fast and efficient response.

How Our Platform Works

A simple 3-step process for a cleaner city, powered by citizens and AI.

1. Complaint Submission

Citizens spot a full bin, snap a photo, and upload it through our simple portal.

2. AI Verification

Our AI instantly analyzes the image to verify the complaint is genuine.

3. Collection & Update

The nearest driver is dispatched, and the status is updated in real-time.
